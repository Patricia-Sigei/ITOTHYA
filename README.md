# Itothya General Agencies LTD

Welcome to the Itothya General Agencies LTD website. This document outlines the structure and functionality of the site, which provides information about our services, policies, and contact details.

## Overview

Itothya General Agencies LTD is a leading agency specializing in debt collection and various related services. Our website is designed to provide potential clients with comprehensive information about our specialties, contact details, and payment options.

## Features

- **Company Overview**: An introduction to Itothya General Agencies LTD, including our slogan.
- **Navigation Bar**: Links to different sections of the website, including "Home," "About Us," "Contact," and "Log In."
- **Services Offered**: Detailed descriptions of the services provided, including debt collection, investigation/skip tracing, debt surveillance, asset searches, consultancy, reconciliation, and process serving.
- **Contact Information**: Clear details on how to reach us, including our physical address, phone numbers, and email.
- **Payment Section**: A form for clients to enter their payment details and submit payments.
- **Policies Section**: Information about our policies and values.

## Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3
- JavaScript
- Google Fonts
- Font Awesome for icons

## Usage

1. Clone the repository to your local machine.
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser to view the website.
3. Navigate through the site using the navigation bar.
