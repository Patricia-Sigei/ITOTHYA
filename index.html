We offer


Debt Recovery


Specialities

  • Consumer debt recovery

    Recovering debts owed by individuals, ensuring a personalized approach to each case.

  • Commercial debt recovery

    Our team is experienced in handling corporate debts, focusing on efficient and effective recovery strategies.


Classifications

  • Impossible: Almost written off.
  • Near Impossible: Maybe recovered debts.
  • Possible: Current debts.

We also offer

Investigation/Skip Tracing

Due to the diversity of our underlying client base and continuous experience in field-based collections, we carry out investigations to trace debtors whom you have lost contact with.

We also serve legal and statutory notices.

Debt Surveillance

We undertake debt surveillance and monitoring of debts that may have been written by companies. Upon confirmation of the changes in the customer's financial circumstances, we duly advise our client.

Asset Searches

Our dedicated team of investigators is trained in asset searching, with the knowledge of various public information databases.

Consultancy

We are consultants in the following areas:

  • Setting up credit policies and system strategies
  • Training on debt collection

Reconciliation

Reconciliation takes up a lot of control time. Therefore, we take the burden and handle it on your behalf. Once you furnish us with data, we work in liaison with your customer, establishing actual amounts before collection.

Process Server

Our experiences, processes, and court servers provide a professional and time-effective service of documents. Whether you have a one process serving requirement or you are looking for a reliable private investigation agency to manage your document volume workloads, we would be happy to help.
